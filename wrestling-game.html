Disaster Zone

The Self-Made Machine, The Glitch, The Nanometer Nightmare

Billed From: A clandestine workshop on the outskirts of Silicon City
Age: 32
Height: 6'2" (188 cm)
Weight: 220 lbs (100 kg)
Alignment: Heel

Character Bio

Disaster Zone, real name Elias Vance, is a chilling embodiment of transhuman ambition gone awry. He wasn't born; he was built. Driven by an obsessive desire for wrestling perfection, Elias sacrificed his natural physique and embraced the cold embrace of technology. He became the Self-Made Machine, a walking testament to the power (and peril) of cybernetic enhancement.

His workshop, a cluttered haven of sparking wires and discarded tech, is his sanctuary and his laboratory. Here, he meticulously crafted his augmentations, pushing the boundaries of bio-mechanical engineering. Micro-servo motors enhance his strength and speed, a neural interface sharpens his reflexes and technical precision, and specialized sensors allow him to analyze his opponents' weaknesses with nanometer accuracy.

Fighting Style

Technical, Submission-based, with elements of Striking and Power. He relies on precision and calculated force rather than brute strength.

Gimmick

Cybernetically enhanced wrestling prodigy. He's cold, calculating, and arrogant, believing himself to be superior to his "unaugmented" opponents. He speaks in technical jargon, often using scientific terms to describe his moves and his opponents' weaknesses.

Mike Loder

The Frontend, Digger

Billed From: Sydney, New South Wales, Australia
Age: 27
Height: 6'4" (183cm)
Weight: 220lbs (100kg)
Alignment: Tweener leaning toward Babyface

Character Bio

From the rugged New South Wales mining town of Cobar, Mike "The Frontend" Loder has carved a reputation as a force to be reckoned with. A FIFO mine worker by trade, Mike spends his days operating heavy machinery and moving tons of earth. But his true passion lies in the squared circle.

Fighting Style

Mike is a powerhouse striker, a bush brawler rather than a trained martial artist. He relies on brute strength and isn't shy of "letting loose a little of the old claret" to get his point across.

Gimmick

The no-nonsense, hard-hitting, Aussie battler. By day, Mike Loder works the mines, moving earth and hauling loads. By night (or whenever he's on his R&R), he unleashes his raw power in the wrestling ring.

Mei Ling Tan

"The Lioness" of Singapore

Billed From: Singapore
Age: 26
Height: 5'3" (160cm)
Weight: 115 lbs. (52kg)
Alignment: Babyface

Character Bio

Mei Ling grew up in a small HDB flat in Toa Payoh, a typical heartland neighborhood in Singapore. Her parents were hardworking hawkers, running a popular chicken rice stall in a nearby hawker center. From a young age, Mei Ling was exposed to the vibrant and diverse culture of Singapore, with its mix of Chinese, Malay, Indian, and Eurasian influences.

Fighting Style

Speed, Cruiserweight style. Mei Ling's greatest assets are her speed and agility. She uses these to outmaneuver her opponents, delivering a flurry of strikes before they can react. She's also adept at using the ropes and turnbuckles to her advantage, launching herself into high-flying attacks.
