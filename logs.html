Campaign Logs

Act I: Shadows of the Past

Mission 1: The Seeds of Curiosity

Initial Contact

The crew was approached by Professor Albericiepr from the university, who offered them a research mission to Drenalch. The professor explained that a team of researchers had gone missing while investigating ancient ruins on the planet.

Arrival on Drenalch

Upon arriving at Drenalch, the crew met with Intendant Voriepr, the postgraduate leader of the research team. They learned about the complex Ghenani bureaucracy and the challenges of obtaining permits for archaeological research.

The Echo Keepers

The crew discovered the existence of the Echo Keepers, a faction dedicated to protecting ancient Ghenani sites. They learned about the "Sleeping Sickness" affecting the local population and the strict laws regarding ancient technology.

Missing Ambassador

The crew investigated the disappearance of Ambassador Voristlas and his assistant, Intendent Lyraviepr. They discovered that Lyraviepr had attempted to return a stolen artifact to the ancient ruins, unaware of the dangerous radiation present at the site.
