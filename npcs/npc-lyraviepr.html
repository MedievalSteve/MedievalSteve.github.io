Intendent Lyraviepr

Zhodani Zhodani Consulate Drenalch Diplomat Act I: Shadows of the Past Mission 1: The Seeds of Curiosity Mission 2: The Shadows of the Past

Assistant to Ambassador Voristlas, Lyraviepr's story is one of misguided intentions and dire consequences. Initially reported missing at the start of the mission, the Ambassador suspected she was 'going native' among the Ghenani.

Role
Diplomatic Assistant
Location
Echo Keeper Custody
Status
Imprisoned

Current Situation

Her true fate was more complex: she was discovered at the Ancient's ruins, attempting to return a stolen artifact. Unaware of the dangerous radiation present at the site, her actions were driven by a desperate attempt to make amends for her theft. She is now in the custody of Echo Keeper Lobon, facing the threat of the death penalty for the theft of Ancient technology.
