HSS Squad Leader Valerius (DECEASED)

Hyperion Security Damrong Mission 3: The Damrong Disappearance Deceased Human

HSS Squad Leader Valerius was a consummate professional, a product of the Hyperion League's elite corporate security program. Ex-military from an independent world absorbed by the League, he chose a lucrative corporate career over fighting a losing battle.

Role
Security Squad Leader
Organization
Hyperion Lines Security
Status
Deceased

Personality

Valerius was driven by a desire for efficiency and a clean record. He despised messy situations and would always seek the path of least resistance to achieve his objective. He was not cruel, but he was pragmatic to a fault; the fate of the IGS scientists was a data point, not a moral concern.

Tactics

Valerius would not open fire unless fired upon. His initial approach was professional intimidation, using his squad's superior numbers and firepower to establish a perimeter and demand compliance. He was open to negotiation, but only if it led to him acquiring the artifact.

Fate

Gunned down in a firefight with the crew, his body was grabbed and dragged onto his ship by his crew. He is presumed deceased.
