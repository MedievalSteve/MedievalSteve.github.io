Kaelen Lupeski

Hyperion Corporate Independent

Kaelen Lupeski is a member of the influential Lupeski family that controls Hyperion Lines and the Hyperion League. As part of the family that has effectively taken over the government of Hyperion, Kaelen represents the corporate interests that dominate the system.

Family Position

As a member of the Lupeski family, Kaelen is part of the corporate dynasty that controls Hyperion Lines and, through it, the Hyperion League. The family's control extends beyond Hyperion to include holdings on multiple worlds.

Personality

Kaelen represents the corporate interests that have effectively replaced traditional government on Hyperion. The family's expansionist policies have created tensions with other systems in the Vanguard Reaches.
