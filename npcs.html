Captain Roric "Ironjaw" Vayne (DECEASED)

Pirate Imperial Human The Nest Deceased Act I: Shadows of the Past Mission 2: The Shadows of the Past

Captain Roric "Ironjaw" Vayne was the ruthless and battle-hardened leader of the Void Vultures. He ruled his pirate gang through a potent combination of fear, cunning, and the promise of immense plunder, demanding absolute loyalty from his subordinates. His authority was unquestioned within The Nest, and his reputation was punctuated by his most distinct physical feature: a crude, menacing cybernetic jaw replacement, the source of his moniker and a constant, visible reminder of his violent past.

Vayne was a tactically astute but brutally direct commander, preferring to overwhelm his enemies with focused, aggressive force. This confidence, however, bordered on arrogance, particularly concerning his flagship, the "Void Reaper", which he considered a flawless extension of his own power. Any perceived slight against his ship or his personal honor was met with disproportionate rage.

Beneath his aggressive exterior lay a deep-seated paranoia. He was notoriously distrustful of modern wireless technology and was especially wary of psionics, which he viewed as a tool for spies and assassins. This forced him to rely on older, hard-wired systems for his most sensitive operations, creating a unique operational vulnerability.

Fate: Vayne was killed in a skirmish on his Space Station when a Stun Grenade he was using went off in his hand and he was executed by Lucius Vin Geitenhoven.
